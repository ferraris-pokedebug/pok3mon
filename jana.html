About

Make sure to know what is this app's purpose :)

KURYENTerest helps you track and reduce your home energy use with easy appliance monitoring and daily tips for saving power. Simply enter appliance details and usage, and see your estimated energy consumption.

The app teaches you about energy use and offers advice to cut waste and switch to cleaner options. It's perfect for students, families, and anyone wanting to save on electricity bills and help the environment.

Make smart energy choices with KURYENTerest and support a sustainable future.
